North Bali Canyoning

Adrenaline, Waterfalls, and Hidden Gorges

Tour Overview

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush deep in the hidden canyons of Gitgit and Sambangan. Rappel down thundering waterfalls, slide through sculpted volcanic rock gorges, and plunge into crystal-clear jungle pools. Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced thrill-seeker, our certified local guides will ensure an unforgettable, safe, and action-packed adventure.

Tour Itinerary

  • 07:00 AM - Hotel Pick-up

    Private transfer from your Bali hotel or villa to the northern highlands.

  • 09:30 AM - Gear Up & Briefing

    Arrive at base camp. Get fitted with your wetsuit, harness, and helmet, followed by a professional safety briefing.

  • 10:00 AM - Canyoning Descent

    Enter the canyon. Rappel down waterfall faces, slide along natural chutes, and leap into emerald pools.

  • 12:00 PM - Swim & Photos

    Enjoy a free swim in a stunning natural pool while your guide captures epic action shots.

  • 01:30 PM - Lunch & Shower

    Return to base camp for a hot shower, followed by a generous traditional Balinese lunch.

  • 04:30 PM - Return to Hotel

    Drop-off back at your accommodation to rest those well-used muscles.
