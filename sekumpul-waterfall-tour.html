Sekumpul Waterfall Trek

Lush Jungles, Hidden Falls, and Northern Valleys

Immerse yourself in the untamed beauty of Northern Bali. Renowned as the most spectacular waterfall on the island, Sekumpul consists of majestic twin cascades plunging 80 meters into a lush jungle ravine. Guided by a local village expert, you will hike through exotic fruit plantations, cross river streams, and discover hidden natural pools perfect for a refreshing swim.

Tour Itinerary

  • 07:00 AM - Hotel Pick-up

    Relax in our private, air-conditioned transport as we drive towards the cooler, mountainous region of Northern Bali.

  • 09:30 AM - Sekumpul Jungle Trek

    Arrive at the village and meet your dedicated local trekking guide. Begin your descent down hundreds of steps through dense tropical foliage and local spice farms.

  • 10:30 AM - Sekumpul & Fiji Waterfalls

    Stand in awe at the base of the massive twin falls. Feel the mist, take incredible photos, and enjoy an invigorating swim in the natural rock pools hidden deeper in the valley.

  • 01:30 PM - Scenic Lunch

    After trekking back up the valley, reward yourself with a delicious traditional Indonesian lunch at a local restaurant overlooking the beautiful landscape.

  • 03:00 PM - Twin Lakes Viewpoint

    On our way back south, we will stop at the stunning panoramic viewpoint overlooking Lake Buyan and Lake Tamblingan in the Bedugul highlands.
