East Nusa Penida Tour

Panoramic Vistas and Iconic Photo Spots

Venture to the spectacular eastern shores of Nusa Penida. This private day trip is designed for those seeking breathtaking coastal views, iconic photo opportunities, and the raw beauty of Bali's neighboring island. From the gleaming white sands of Diamond Beach to the famous Molenteng Tree House, our experienced team ensures a comfortable, safe, and truly memorable journey.

Tour Itinerary

  • 06:30 AM - Hotel Pick-up

    Private transfer from your Bali hotel to Sanur Harbor.

  • 07:30 AM - Fast Boat to Penida

    High-speed boat transfer across the beautiful Badung Strait.

  • 09:30 AM - Atuh Beach

    Arrive and explore the beautiful, secluded Atuh Beach.

  • 10:30 AM - Thousand Island Viewpoint

    Take in the breathtaking Raja Lima panoramic ocean vistas.

  • 11:30 AM - Molenteng Tree House

    Visit the famous Rumah Pohon for an iconic vacation photo.

  • 01:30 PM - Lunch

    Enjoy a delicious local lunch featuring Balinese or Western foods.

  • 02:30 PM - Diamond Beach

    Walk along the stunning white sands and towering karst formations.

  • 05:30 PM - Return to Bali

    Fast boat ride back and drop-off at your hotel.
