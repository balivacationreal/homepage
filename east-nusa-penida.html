East Nusa Penida Tour

Tour Overview

Venture to the spectacular eastern shores of Nusa Penida. This private day trip is designed for those seeking breathtaking coastal views, iconic photo opportunities, and the raw beauty of Bali's neighboring island.

From the gleaming white sands of Diamond Beach to the famous Molenteng Tree House, our experienced team ensures a comfortable, safe, and truly memorable journey for you and your group.

What's Included

  • Private round-trip transportation from your hotel
  • Round-trip fast boat tickets
  • Private A/C car on the island
  • All entrance fees to attractions
  • English-speaking driver/guide
  • Authentic Balinese lunch

Itinerary Schedule

  • 06:30 AM Hotel pick-up and transfer to Sanur Harbor.
  • 07:30 AM Hop on the high-speed boat to Nusa Penida.
  • 09:30 AM Arrive at and explore the beautiful Atuh Beach.
  • 10:30 AM Take in the breathtaking Thousand Island Viewpoint.
  • 11:30 AM Visit the famous Rumah Pohon (Molenteng Tree House).
  • 01:30 PM Enjoy an authentic Balinese lunch.
  • 02:30 PM Walk along the stunning white sands of Diamond Beach.
  • 05:30 PM Return to your hotel in Bali.
