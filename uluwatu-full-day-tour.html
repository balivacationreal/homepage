Uluwatu Full Day Tour

Sunsets, Culture, and Coastal Splendor

Discover the magic of South Bali with our exclusive, all-inclusive Uluwatu Full Day Tour. This carefully crafted itinerary combines stunning coastal scenery, rich cultural heritage, and a mesmerizing sunset experience. From towering statues to cliffside temples and romantic beachfront dining, this tour showcases the absolute best of the Bukit Peninsula.

Tour Itinerary

  • 1. GWK Cultural Park

    Begin your journey at the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park. Marvel at the colossal, 122-meter-tall statue of Lord Vishnu riding the mythical bird Garuda, a breathtaking symbol of Indonesian heritage and incredible architectural engineering.

  • 2. Padang Padang Beach

    Relax on the pristine white sands of this world-famous surfing beach. Tucked beneath dramatic limestone cliffs and accessed via a unique hollow rock entrance, this picturesque cove gained global fame after being featured in the movie 'Eat Pray Love'.

  • 3. Pura Luhur Uluwatu (Temple)

    Explore one of Bali's most spectacular spiritual pillars. Perched proudly on a steep cliff 70 meters above the roaring Indian Ocean, Uluwatu Temple offers panoramic vistas. Keep an eye out for the mischievous resident macaque monkeys who guard the temple grounds!

  • 4. Kecak & Fire Dance Performance

    As the sun begins to set over the ocean, take your seat in the open-air amphitheater. Experience an unforgettable performance where dozens of men rhythmically chant the epic Hindu Ramayana story around a blazing fire, without the use of any musical instruments.

  • 5. Jimbaran Bay Seafood Dinner

    Conclude your perfect day under the stars. Sit at a candlelit table directly on the sandy shores of Jimbaran Beach and indulge in a mouth-watering, freshly grilled seafood dinner accompanied by the gentle sound of the waves.
