Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At Bali Real Vacation, protecting your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website (www.balirealvacation.com) and book our travel services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide you with a seamless travel experience. When you use our booking form, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your WhatsApp number
  • Travel details (selected package, dates, and number of people)
  • Any special requests or notes you provide

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To process your booking requests and arrange your travel itineraries.
  • To communicate with you via WhatsApp regarding your trip details, pick-up times, and customer support.
  • To ensure our local drivers and guides are prepared for any special requirements you noted.

3. Information Sharing

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your information is only shared internally with our team and the specific drivers assigned to your tour to ensure a smooth operation.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us directly via our official WhatsApp number: +62 812-3915-6586.
